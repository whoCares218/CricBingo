About Cricket Bingo

Cricket Bingo is a free, browser-based IPL cricket quiz game inspired by the popular Immaculate Grid format. Players are shown the name of a famous IPL cricketer and must tap the correct cell on a bingo-style grid — matching that player to their IPL team, nationality, or trophy. You have 10 seconds per player. How many can you get right?

🎮 How to Play

  • A cricketer's name appears at the top of the screen
  • The grid shows IPL teams, nations, or trophies
  • Tap the cell that correctly matches the player
  • Each question has a 10-second timer
  • Fill all cells to earn a 200-point grid bonus
  • Use Wildcard to auto-fill matching cells (−20 pts)
  • Skip any player with no score change

🎯 Difficulty Levels

Cricket Bingo has three difficulty settings, each changing the mix of players and grid categories:

  • 🟢 Easy — Teams only. 75% star players, 25% well-known players. Best for beginners.
  • 🟡 Normal — Teams & Nations. 50/50 split of star and known players. The balanced challenge.
  • 🔴 Hard — Teams, Nations & Combo cells. Includes obscure players. For cricket experts only.

📅 Daily Challenge

Every day, Cricket Bingo generates a new Daily Challenge board — seeded from today's date. This means every player in the world gets exactly the same puzzle on the same day. Compare your score with friends and see who knows their IPL cricket better. The daily board resets at midnight UTC.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cricket Bingo?

Cricket Bingo is a free, browser-based IPL cricket trivia game. It presents you with a bingo-style grid of IPL teams, nations, and trophies. You are shown one player at a time and must identify the correct matching cell before a 10-second timer expires.

Is Cricket Bingo free to play?

Yes, completely free. No account is required, no downloads, and no payment. Just open the page and start playing instantly in your browser.

How many IPL players are in the game?

The overall pool covers 500+ players spanning the entire history of the Indian Premier League from 2008 to 2026. The IPL 2026 Edition uses only players from the current season's registered squads.

What are Combo cells on Hard difficulty?

Combo cells combine two attributes — for example, "Mumbai Indians + India" requires you to find a player who has played for Mumbai Indians AND is from India, or "Chennai Super Kings + IPL" for a player who won the IPL trophy with CSK. These are the hardest cell types in the game.

Does the game save my scores?

Yes. Your scores, stats, and history are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No data is sent to any server. You can view your full history and personal bests by clicking the 📊 icon in the top navigation bar.

How is the Daily Challenge different from a regular game?

The Daily Challenge uses a date-based seed to generate the same board for every player worldwide on that day. Your daily score represents your performance on that specific shared puzzle, making it easy to compare with friends.

I found a player data error. How do I report it?

Use the Contact page and select "Player / Data Error" as the subject. We review and fix errors quickly. You can also email us directly at tehm8111@gmail.com.
