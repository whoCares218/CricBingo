About Cricket Bingo

Cricket Bingo is a free browser-based cricket quiz where you match famous cricketers to their IPL franchises, nationalities, and trophies on a bingo-style grid.

Players are shown one by one — tap the correct matching cell before the 10-second timer expires. Score points for accuracy and speed. Take the daily challenge for a shared board with everyone worldwide.

25 players are selected per game using a fame-based distribution, then grid categories are built from those 25 — guaranteeing every cell always has a valid answer.
